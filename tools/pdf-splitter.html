Understanding PDF Splitting

PDF splitting is the process of dividing a single PDF document into multiple separate files. This tool allows you to extract specific pages or ranges of pages from a PDF, creating new individual documents from the original file. Whether you need to separate chapters from a large document, extract specific pages for sharing, or split a multi-page PDF into individual files, our PDF splitter provides a simple, secure, and efficient solution.

Our PDF splitter processes all files locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security. Your documents never leave your device, and no files are uploaded to any server. This client-side processing means your sensitive documents remain confidential while still providing professional-quality results. The split PDFs maintain the original formatting, images, text, and quality of the source document.

The tool supports multiple splitting methods: split by page ranges (e.g., pages 1-10, 11-20), split every N pages (e.g., every 5 pages), or split into individual pages (one file per page). You can also extract specific pages or ranges and download them as separate files. Once split, all resulting PDFs are packaged in a ZIP file for easy download and organization.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Will splitting affect the quality of my PDF?

No, the PDF splitter preserves the original quality of all pages. Images, text, formatting, and other elements remain exactly as they appear in the source file. Each split PDF maintains the same resolution and quality as the original.

Can I split password-protected PDFs?

You'll need to unlock password-protected PDFs first using our PDF Password Remover tool. Once unlocked, you can split them normally. The tool cannot process encrypted PDFs directly.

What's the maximum file size I can split?

File size limits depend on your device's memory since all processing happens locally. You can typically split PDFs up to 100-200MB without issues. Very large files may require more memory or take longer to process.

Are my PDFs safe and private?

Absolutely! All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your PDFs never leave your computer, are not uploaded to any server, and are not stored anywhere. Your privacy and security are guaranteed.

Can I split a PDF into specific page ranges?

Yes, you can specify exact page ranges for splitting. For example, you can split pages 1-10 into one file, pages 11-20 into another, and so on. The tool supports custom ranges for flexible splitting options.
